## Party Recommendations

### General Tips

1. **All Level 80+** - Below this is suicide
2. **Fully Equipped** - Legendary/Mythic gear
3. **Max Skills** - Skill proficiency matters
4. **Strong Relationships** - Intervene can save runs
5. **Consumables** - Superior potions, buff elixirs

### Per Modifier

| Modifier | Recommended Party |

## Tips for Success

### Preparation

- Check modifier BEFORE entering
- Adjust party for specific challenge
- Bring appropriate consumables
- Discuss strategy if co-op

### During the Dungeon

- Don't rush into rooms blindly
- Save cooldowns for key moments
- Retreat if things go badly (live to try again)
- Watch the enrage timer if applicable

### After Completion

- Equip new Ancestral immediately
- Check for build-defining uniques
- Plan next week based on experience

## Common Mistakes

1. **Underleveled Heroes** - 80 is minimum, not optimal
2. **Ignoring Modifier** - Each requires different approach
3. **No Consumables** - You'll need them
4. **Solo Tank** - Bring backup for most modifiers
5. **Giving Up** - Practice makes perfect
