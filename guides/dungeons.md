## Dungeon Environments

Seven distinct environments, each with unique enemies and hazards:

### Forest

- **Enemies:** Beasts, Humanoids (bandits, goblins)
- **Hazards:** Overgrowth, wild animal ambushes
- **Loot Focus:** Herbs, leather, wood
- **Tips:** Bring a Ranger for tracking

### Cave

- **Enemies:** Beasts, Humanoids (orcs, trolls)
- **Hazards:** Darkness, cramped spaces
- **Loot Focus:** Ore, gems
- **Tips:** Need light sources or suffer penalties

### Ruins

- **Enemies:** Undead, Constructs
- **Hazards:** Traps, unstable structures
- **Loot Focus:** Artifacts, ancient items
- **Tips:** Bring a Rogue for trap detection

### Crypt

- **Enemies:** Undead (skeletons, vampires, liches)
- **Hazards:** Darkness, cursed ground
- **Loot Focus:** Gold, cursed items
- **Tips:** Clerics excel here (anti-undead)

### Swamp

- **Enemies:** Beasts, Undead
- **Hazards:** Poison, difficult terrain, ambushes
- **Loot Focus:** Rare herbs, exotic materials
- **Tips:** Bring antidotes and high VIT heroes

### Tower

- **Enemies:** Constructs, Elementals
- **Hazards:** Magic traps, arcane puzzles
- **Loot Focus:** Magic items, arcane materials
- **Tips:** High INT helps with puzzles

### Volcano

- **Enemies:** Demons, Fire Elementals
- **Hazards:** Extreme heat, lava
- **Loot Focus:** Fire materials, demon parts
- **Tips:** Fire resistance is essential

## Expedition Types

### Supervised Expeditions

You watch the dungeon crawl in real-time (one per night).

**Benefits:**
- +25% XP bonus
- +20% loot quality
- +20 to injury rolls (safer)
- Can order retreat to save heroes
- Use Command Points for interventions

**Command Points:**

| Action | Cost | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| Use Item | 1 | Apply consumable |
| Change Tactics | 1 | Shift strategy |
| Focus Fire | 1 | All attack same target |
| Defensive Formation | 1 | Reduce damage |
| Activate Skill | 2 | Trigger hero ability |
| Search Area | 2 | Find hidden items |
| Emergency Retreat | 3 | Abort, save party |

Start with 5 + stars. Regenerate 1 per room cleared.

### Unsupervised Expeditions

Auto-resolved based on party strength and tactical preset.

**Tactical Presets:**

| Preset | Risk | Reward | Behavior |
|--------|------|--------|----------|
| Reckless | Very High | Very High | Never retreat |
| Aggressive | High | High | Minimal caution |
| Balanced | Medium | Medium | Standard |
| Cautious | Low | Low | Avoid fights |
| Survival | Very Low | Very Low | Abort at danger |

## Party Formation

### Party Size

- **Minimum:** 1 hero (dangerous!)
- **Maximum:** 8 heroes
- **Recommended:** Stars + 2 (e.g., ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê = 5 heroes)

### Building a Good Party

1. **Tank** - Warrior or Cleric to absorb damage
2. **Healer** - Cleric to keep everyone alive
3. **DPS** - Mage, Rogue, Ranger for damage
4. **Utility** - Rogue for traps, Ranger for scouting

### Relationship Synergy

- **Friends:** +damage and combat bonuses
- **Enemies:** -damage and may refuse to help
- **Lovers:** Big bonuses, but devastating if one dies

### Pre-Mission Checklist

- [ ] Heroes are Ready (not injured/crafting)
- [ ] Mood is good (not exhausted/angry)
- [ ] Equipment is appropriate level
- [ ] Consumables equipped (potions!)
- [ ] Class balance (tank, heal, dps)

## Death and Injury

### When Heroes Hit 0 HP

A **death save** determines survival:

```
Base: 50%
+ VIT √ó 1%
+ LCK √ó 0.5%
+ Supervised: +20%
+ Cleric in party: +10% each
+ Ally intervene: May take hit instead
```

### Injury Severity

| Severity | Duration | Penalty |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Minor | 1-3 days | -5% stats |
| Moderate | 3-10 days | -10% stats |
| Severe | 10-30 days | -20% stats |
| Critical | 10+ days | -30% stats |

Injuries require rest or medical treatment at the Infirmary.
