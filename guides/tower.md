# Abyssal Spire (Tower)

The Abyssal Spire is an endless dungeon challenge where you climb floor after floor against increasingly difficult enemies. How high can your guild reach?

## Unlocking the Spire

**Requirements:**
- Hero Level: 95+ (at least one hero)
- Guild Rank: A or higher
- Access: Mission Board → Abyssal Spire

---

## Tower Structure

### Floor Progression

The Spire has unlimited floors, each harder than the last:

| Floor Range | Enemies | Difficulty |
|-------------|---------|------------|
| 1-20 | 1-3 | Early game |
| 21-50 | 2-4 | Mid game |
| 51-100 | 3-5 | Late game |
| 100+ | 4-6 | Extreme |

### Floor Types

| Floor | Event |
|-------|-------|
| Every floor | Combat encounter |
| Every 10 | Mini-boss |
| Every 15 | Shop |
| Every 25 | Major boss |

---

## Enemy Scaling

Enemies scale with floor number:

```
Enemy Level = Base + Floor Number
Enemy HP = Base HP × (1 + Floor × 0.05)
Enemy Damage = Base Damage × (1 + Floor × 0.03)
```

By floor 100, enemies have +500% HP and +300% damage!

### Enemy Count by Floor

| Floor Tier | Min Enemies | Max Enemies |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| 1-20 | 1 | 3 |
| 21-50 | 2 | 4 |
| 51-100 | 3 | 5 |
| 100+ | 4 | 6 |

## Shops (Every 15 Floors)

Wandering merchants appear every 15 floors:

### Shop Contents

- Health/mana potions
- Buff items
- Equipment (scales with floor)
- Skill gems (rare)
- Materials

### Pricing

Prices scale with floor depth:
- Floor 15: Normal prices
- Floor 30: +50% prices
- Floor 45: +100% prices
- Floor 60+: +150% prices

### Strategy

- Save gold for higher floors
- Prioritize consumables
- Check for build-completing items
- Don't overspend early

## Progression System

### Saving Progress

- Progress saves after each floor
- Can resume from last completed floor
- Reset available (start from floor 1)
- Leaderboard tracks highest floor

### Attempt Limits

- One active Spire attempt per guild
- Heroes locked until run ends
- Run ends on party wipe
- Can retreat to save progress

### Retreating

You can retreat at any time:
- Keep all loot gained so far
- Progress saved to current floor
- Party returns safely
- Can resume later

---

## Strategy Guide

### Party Composition

**Recommended:**
- 1-2 Tanks (Warriors, Templars)
- 1-2 Healers (Clerics)
- 2-3 DPS (mix of classes)
- Consider: Necromancer minions for fodder

**Floor 50+:**
- Max level heroes only
- Best equipment possible
- Strong relationships for intervene

### Resource Management

1. **Conserve potions early** - Floors 1-20 are easy
2. **Use shops wisely** - Save gold for later
3. **Retreat before wipe** - Keep your progress
4. **Buff before bosses** - Every advantage matters

### When to Push vs. Retreat

**Push if:**
- Party HP above 70%
- Consumables available
- No heroes at death's door
- Boss coming (better rewards)

**Retreat if:**
- Multiple heroes injured
- Out of consumables
- Been going too long
- Tank struggling

## Tips for High Floors

### Floor 1-50

- Standard party works fine
- Learn enemy patterns
- Practice boss mechanics
- Build gold reserves

### Floor 51-100

- Fully optimized builds required
- Use all consumables
- Perfect execution needed

- One mistake = wipe
